# Murim Chronicles - Combat Trauma Character Modifiers
# Timed modifiers applied after duels and combat events
# Severity scales: sparring (minor) < honor duel (moderate) < death match (severe) < ambush (unpredictable)
# Winners roll at 50% of loser injury chance - winners CAN get hurt
# Repeated fights within 30 days STACK injury risk via recent_combat tiers
# Load-safe: all modifiers have graceful degradation

######################################

# Applied after ANY duel/combat. Stacks increase injury chance in next fight.
# Tier 1: fought within 30 days. Tier 2: fought twice in 30 days. Tier 3: three+ fights in 30 days.
